How to Evaluate AI Tools

A quick 4-step checklist for selecting tools that actually fit your needs

01

Define Your Goal

Choose one clear use case: writing, coding, image generation, support, or research.

02

Compare Output Quality

Test real prompts and check quality, speed, consistency, and hallucination risk.

03

Review Cost and Privacy

Inspect pricing plans, API limits, data retention policies, and team permissions.

04

Run a Small Pilot

Use a 7-day trial with actual tasks, then keep only tools that improve outcomes.
